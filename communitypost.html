What breed of dog would be most suited for me?

I live in a small apartment. I’ve always been looking for a companion to complete my family, but I’m worried that my pet wouldn’t like my home due to it’s size. So I want to know dog breeds that are suited for a small space. My apartment has a swimming area and is near a dog park. In addition, I work from home so my hours are more flexible and I can take my pet to the park almost everyday. So any suggestions?

REPLY

Marco Harrison

A Minute Ago

Given your living situation and lifestyle, two dog breeds that are well-suited for small spaces are the French Bulldog and the Cavalier King Charles Spaniel. Both breeds are small in size, adaptable to apartment living, and generally get along well with their owners and other dogs
