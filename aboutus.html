About

Real Mate

Welcome to Real Mate, the premier online platform connecting animal breeders, adoption centers, and animal lovers of all kinds. Our website serves as a bridge, enabling seamless connections between breeders and adoption centers with potential buyers and adopters looking for their perfect companion across a diverse range of species.


From adorable puppies and kittens to exotic birds, small mammals, reptiles, and more, our platform showcases a wide variety of animals available for adoption or purchase. We prioritize the welfare and ethical treatment of animals, ensuring that all breeders and adoption centers featured on our website undergo a rigorous verification process to ensure their credibility and responsible practices.

VISION

Our vision is to create a dynamic and inclusive space where individuals seeking a companion can easily connect with reputable breeders, sellers, and adoption centers, ensuring a seamless and responsible adoption process.


Through our website, we aim to empower adopters and buyers with comprehensive information about various breeds, health records, and the background of the pets available. We believe in promoting transparency and ethics, providing a trustworthy environment for both parties involved. Our platform fosters meaningful connections, enabling adopters and buyers to find the perfect pet that aligns with their preferences and lifestyle. We strive to make the adoption process more efficient, convenient, and secure.

MISSION

Our mission is to revolutionize the pet adoption process by creating a user-friendly platform that connects breeders, sellers, adoption centers, and adopters/buyers. We prioritize the welfare of animals and promote responsible pet ownership through education and awareness.


By fostering a compassionate community, we aim to simplify the search for a companion while advocating for spaying/neutering, vaccination, and proper care. Together, we can make a positive impact on the lives of animals, promoting happier and healthier human-animal relationships.
